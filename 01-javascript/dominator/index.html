The Dominator

Great location but the food was a little disappointing. Had the Nasi goreng and it was not very flavourful and the beef rendang I got was very chewy meaning it was overbooked instead of slowly cooked or it was left overs from a couple of days ago. The service is good here though and the food is cheap, really cheap and affordable. If I was short on cash/ saving money I would go here to get a cheap fix.
